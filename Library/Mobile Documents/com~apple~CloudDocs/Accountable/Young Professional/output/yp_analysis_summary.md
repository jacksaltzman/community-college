## Methodology

### Data Source

American Community Survey (ACS) 5-year estimates, 2022 vintage (2018-2022),
pulled via Census Bureau API at the congressional district level (CD118, 118th Congress).

### Persona Definition

| Filter | Definition | ACS Table | Age Bracket |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Age | 25-34 | B01001 | 25-29, 30-34 |
| Education | Bachelor's degree or higher | B15001 | 25-34 |
| Employment | Civilian employed | B23001 | 25-29, 30-34 |
| Income | $40K-$200K household income | B19037 | 25-44 (householder) |
| Enrollment | Not enrolled in college/grad school | B14004 | 25-34 |

### Estimation Method

Stepwise proportional estimation applied per-district:

```
yp_estimate = pop_25_34 x pct_bachelors x pct_employed x pct_income_40k_200k x pct_not_enrolled
```

Each proportion is district-specific. This assumes approximate independence between filters.

### Caveats

1. **Age approximation**: ACS brackets give 25-34; spec requested 22-35.
2. **Independence assumption**: Filters are applied multiplicatively, assuming independence. Education and employment are positively correlated, so this may slightly overcount.
3. **Income table age bracket**: B19037 uses householder age 25-44, not 25-34. This includes older householders (35-44) in the income proportion.
4. **Householder vs individual**: B19037 measures household income by householder age. YPs in shared housing where someone else is the householder may be undercounted.
5. **ACS margins of error**: Small districts may have significant sampling error. Estimates should be treated as approximations.
